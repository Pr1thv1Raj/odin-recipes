Paneer butter masala is a rich and creamy curry made with paneer,spices, ,tomatoes,onions and butter. It is served piping hot with naans or rotis or parathas.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams Paneer
  • 2 diced onions
  • 2 pureed tomatoes
  • Dry spices
  • Butter
  • Everest paneer masala mix
  • Stove
  • Will to live
