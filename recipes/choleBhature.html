Chole Bhature

Chole bhature is not a single word. It is an emotion. Chole means chickpeas of the best caliber whereas bhature or bhatura (singular Bhatura) is fried puffy dough. This is the leading cause of cholestrol imbalance in India.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams chole
  • 2 diced onions
  • 2 pureed tomatoes
  • Dry spices
  • Butter
  • Everest chana masala mix
  • Dough
  • Stove
  • Will to live

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove
  2. Then realize it's too much effort to cook and you dont have time and also you lack most of the ingredients
  3. Order from swiggy or zomato or uber eats
  4. Wait for an eternity
  5. Enjoy your cold chole and soggy Bhaturas and pretend that it is the second best thing after paneer butter masala that you ever put in your mouth so as justifies its price
